Why It Matters

Diseases like Black Sigatoka and Panama Disease devastate banana plantations worldwide, leading to massive yield losses and economic hardship for farmers. Early detection is crucial, but manual inspection is slow and error-prone.

How AI Solves It

By analyzing microscopic patterns, discoloration, and structural changes on the leaf surface, our advanced machine learning models can detect diseases before they become visible to the naked eye. We use a K-Nearest Neighbors classifier trained on hundreds of verified samples.
